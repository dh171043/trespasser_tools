Blood
You study biological applications of magic, manipulating
the very essence of life that runs in the veins of living
creatures. When used benevolently, your magic can
restore vitality and cure disease. When used for ill
purposes, it is among the greatest profanities of nature.
Most blood deeds have the spell keyword, meaning they
must be used with a bare hand or an implement, such as a
wand, rod, or staff. Practitioners of blood magic excel at
restoring hit points, but unlike other healers, they do not
grant recoveries, instead restoring hit points directly
from the damage they deal.
Key Attribute: Might
Your magic is drawn from your own living essence.
When you take up Blood as your first craft, set your key
attribute to MIGHT. When you take it up at higher levels,
you may choose to change it to MIGHT or keep it the same.
Blood Manipulation
Owing to your control over the living essence, you can
create original effects using yours and other creatures'
blood as a basis. By focusing your power, you can detect
the heart rate of other creatures, speed or slow your own
heart rate, raise or lower your body temperature, or create
other blood-related effects at the discretion of the Judge.
When you test the limits of your abilities with this
feature, the Judge may call for a MIGHT | MAGIC check to
determine the efficacy.
Life Affinity
Your study of lifeblood empowers your magic. When you
make a casting check for any incantation or ritual that
involves manipulating blood or affecting the energy of
life by some other means, gain an extra spark.
